Distribution Board

A distribution board, also known as a panel board is a part of an electrical supply, dividing the power electricity into sections inside a box. With new electricity technology, distribution boards are as much needed in the internet infrastructure.

Manhole

Manhole covers can be found on the street ground. Underneath the manhole covers, you can find cables providing connections to the network from suburb to suburb and city to city.

CCTV

CCTV, or closed-circuit television is a recording video camera set to monitor a specific place to track and refer to if there has been an event that happened.You can find CCT cameras in most shops and homes around Wellington but some are hidden.

Satellite Dish

Used all around Wellington and over to the other side of the world, a satellite dish is a type of antenna designed to receive or transmit information by radio waves to or from a broadcasting satellite.

Antenna

Antennas work as a wireless server used with a transmitter sending signals, which is intercepted by a receiver, providing access to mostly television, radio and is now becoming correlated with the internet.
